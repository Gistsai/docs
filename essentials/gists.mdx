## Prompts are the new source codes

Source codes have always held immense value for tech companies due to their ability to streamline tasks and increase efficiency.

With LLMs, anyone can automate tasks by formulating prompts and integrating them into workflows using NoCode tools.

## Gist types

Here are some of the most common task types people use LLMs for:

- Classification
- Information extraction
- Question answering
- Summarization
- Editing
- Brainstorming
- Translation
